About Us

IS117 Home Improvement LLC is a Home Improvement company that seeks to help home-owners make their homes a better place.

We offer a variety of different services, all with customizations to do how the customer wants.

We strive to make our customers as content as could be with our projects.

We have been in business since 1973, and have served thousands of customers since our inception.

Our services include everything from landscaping, painting, artificial ponds, home additions, sewage tank installation, fiber optic instillation, and so much more!
